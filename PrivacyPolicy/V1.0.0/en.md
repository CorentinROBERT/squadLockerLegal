# Privacy Policy - SquadLocker / Le Vestiaire

**Last updated: June 30, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to **SquadLocker**, an application dedicated to sports team management.

Protecting your privacy is a priority for us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our SquadLocker mobile application.

By using SquadLocker, you accept the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Data Collected

### 2.1 Data You Provide Directly

When using the application, we collect the following information:

#### Account Information

- First and last name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Profile photo (optional)
- Account creation date

#### Sports Information

- Teams created or joined
- Team compositions
- Match information (scores, events, statistics)
- Quiz participation
- Results and performance

#### Communication Data

- Messages and interactions within teams
- Notifications and preferences
- Feedback and comments

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Data

#### Technical Data

- Device type and model
- Operating system (iOS, Android)
- Application version
- Unique device identifier
- IP address
- Usage and crash logs

#### Usage Data

- Pages and features accessed
- Time spent in the application
- Interactions with different sections
- Frequency of use

#### Location Data

- Approximate location (if you authorize it)
- Used only to suggest match locations

### 2.3 Third-Party Data

#### Firebase and Google Services

- Notification tokens (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
- Firebase installation identifiers
- Anonymized analytics data

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

- **Contract execution**: to provide application services
- **Consent**: for sending notifications, location
- **Legitimate interest**: to improve the application, ensure security
- **Legal obligation**: to comply with regulatory requirements

Your personal data is used for:

### 4.1 Service Provision

- Create and manage your user account
- Enable team creation and management
- Organize and track matches
- Display scores and statistics
- Manage team compositions
- Facilitate quiz participation

### 4.2 Communication

- Send push notifications regarding:
  - New matches and updates
  - Team invitations
  - Available quizzes

  - Important reminders

### 4.3 Application Improvement

- Analyze usage to improve features

### 4.4 Security and Prevention

- Protect against fraudulent use
- Detect and prevent abuse

## 5. Data Sharing

### 5.1 We Never Sell Your Data

SquadLocker does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

### 5.2 Sharing with Other Users

Certain information is visible to your team members:

- Name and profile photo
- Match statistics (according to visibility settings)
- Quiz participation (after closing)
- Presence in team compositions

### 5.3 Service Providers

We share your data with third-party providers who help us deliver our services:

#### Firebase (Google)

- **Service**: Cloud infrastructure, push notifications, analytics
- **Data shared**: Usage data, notification tokens
- **Location**: Servers in EU and United States
- **Protection**: Privacy Shield certified, GDPR compliant

#### Hosting Services

- **Service**: API and database hosting
- **Data shared**: Account data, teams, matches
- **Protection**: Secure servers with encryption

### 5.4 Legal Obligations

We may disclose your data if:

- Required by law or competent authority
- Necessary to protect our legal rights
- To prevent fraud or imminent danger

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Retention Period

- **Active account**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted account**: Data deleted within 30 days
- **Analytics data**: Anonymized data retained up to 24 months
- **Technical logs**: Retained for 12 months maximum

### 6.2 Legal Archiving

Some data may be retained longer to:

- Comply with our legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our contractual rights

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

### Technical Measures

- ✅ Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- ✅ Password encryption (secure hashing)
- ✅ Secure authentication
- ✅ Firewall and intrusion protection
- ✅ Threat monitoring and detection

### Organizational Measures

- ✅ Limited access to personal data
- ✅ Staff training on data protection
- ✅ Incident response procedures

**Note**: No system is completely secure. We recommend choosing a strong password and not sharing it.

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right of Access

You can request a copy of your personal data.

**How?** Settings > Account > Download my data

### 8.2 Right to Rectification

You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

**How?** Settings > Profile > Edit

### 8.3 Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your data.

**How?** Settings > Account > Delete my account

### 8.4 Right to Data Portability

You can receive your data in a structured format.

**How?** Settings > Account > Export my data

### 8.5 Right to Object

You can object to the processing of your data.

**How?** Contact us through the application

### 8.6 Right to Restriction

You can request restriction of processing.

**How?** Contact us through the application

### 8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time.

**How?** Settings > Privacy

### 8.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You can file a complaint with your supervisory authority:

- **EU**: Your local Data Protection Authority
- **France**: CNIL - https://www.cnil.fr
- **UK**: ICO - https://ico.org.uk
